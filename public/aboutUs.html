SEAN BOLGER

An exceptional running and fitness trainer who is passionate about helping individuals achieve their health and wellness goals. With a deep-rooted love for running and training, Sean possesses the knowledge and expertise needed to guide you on your fitness journey. As an avid runner, Sean Bolger understands the transformative power of running. They recognize that it goes beyond mere exercise, offering an opportunity for personal growth and empowerment. With this in mind, they inspire and motivate clients to embrace running as a means to improve both their physical and mental well-being.

At Sean's core, there is a commitment to providing personalized training experiences. They take the time to understand your unique needs, challenges, and aspirations, tailoring workout plans that suit your fitness level and lifestyle. With their guidance, you'll embark on a comprehensive training program that combines running with cross-training activities to boost endurance, strength, and flexibility.

Welcome to Train Station



At Tran Station, we believe in the power of personalized training to help individuals achieve their fitness goals. We are a small gym dedicated to providing top-quality personal classes in a supportive and motivating environment. Whether you're a beginner looking to kickstart your fitness journey or an experienced athlete aiming to take your performance to the next level, we have the expertise and resources to help you succeed.

Our team of experienced and certified trainers is passionate about helping you unlock your full potential. We understand that every person is unique, with different strengths, abilities, and aspirations. That's why we offer tailored training programs that are specifically designed to meet your individual needs and goals. Our trainers will work closely with you, providing guidance, support, and personalized attention every step of the way.
